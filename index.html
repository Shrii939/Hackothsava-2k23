FAQ

  • What is a hackathon?

    A hackathon is an event where participants come together to collaborate on a project, usually involving coding or software development, over a set period of time. The goal is to create a working prototype or product by the end of the event.

  • How to register for the hackathon?

    Go through the rules, then you need to download the synopsis, then submit it in the registration-form

  • What is the Mode of Hackathon?

    It's a offline event. Location for the Event venue : Shri Madhwa Vadirja Institute of technology and Management, Vishwothama Nagar, Bantakal - 574115

  • Is there any registration fee for the hackathon?

    There is no registration fee

  • What is the duration of the hackathon?

    It's a 24 hour hackathon.

  • What should I bring to a hackathon?

    It's always a good idea to bring your own laptop or computer, as well as any necessary chargers and cables. You may also want to bring a notebook and pen for taking notes or sketching out ideas. Snacks and water are also recommended, as hackathons can be intense and may not have food available at all times.
