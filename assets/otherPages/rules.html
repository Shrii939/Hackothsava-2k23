HACKOTHSAV 2k23

  • HACKOTHSAV is open to all UG and PG students interested in coding.
  • Please Download the synopsis, make any necessary edits, and then provide a link to access the updated version.
  • Maximum of four members in a team.
  • Teams must have all members from the same college. However, members can be from different branches of study.
  • Registration is completely free.
  • The two broad areas for this event are “Transformative education” and “Sustainable Industrialization”. Teams must choose a broad area and submit the proposal during registration itself.
  • Every team will be assigned to a mentor and is needed to submit their progress to their mentor in each phase.
  • Teams should develop the submitted proposal from scratch.
  • Teams must do a minimum of 3 commits on GitHub
  • The judges' decision will be final.
  • Teams must be present in the campus within 9:00 am on 2 June
  • Important date :
    • Submission of the synopsis : 20 May 2023.
    • Intimation of the acceptance : 23 May 2023.
    • Last Date for Registration: 27 May 2023.
    • 02-June-2023 at 11 AM the hackathon starts
    • 03-June-2023 at 11 AM the hackathon Ends
