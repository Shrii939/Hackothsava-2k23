Theme-1

  • Transformative education is an approach to learning that focuses on personal and social transformation. The goal of transformative education is to empower learners to critically examine their own assumptions, beliefs, and values, and to use this self-reflection to challenge and transform the social structures and cultural norms that shape their lives.
  • Transformative education seeks to create a more equitable and just society by encouraging learners to question dominant power structures, recognize and challenge biases and inequalities, and develop the skills and knowledge necessary to effect positive change in their communities.
  • It is an education that encourages learners to become agents of change, to think critically about the world around them, and to take action to create a more just and sustainable future. It emphasizes the importance of experiential learning, critical thinking, and reflection, and it aims to produce learners who are active and engaged citizens of the world.
